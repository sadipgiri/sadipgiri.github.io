WHO IS SADIP?


Hola, I'm a junior at Bennington College, VT, where I'm studying data science and software development. I fell victim to the magic of technology, and the importance of mathematics in it at a very young age of eight and even today my passion for it is more ablaze than ever. When I look back and take a retrospective view of how my learning process has been so far, I see a transformation of myself. The entirety of what I’ve learned, inside or outside the class, can never fit into some neatly enclosed package that would explain my experience but it is becoming increasingly easier to see the whole picture of where my life is directed towards.

Given the vastness of the current tech-realm, I find it necessary to be extremely versatile at this point in time. Right now, I've not thought about reinventing myself as an expert in one particular niche, but I continue to hone my programming as well as mathematical skills. For me, one of the joys associated with being a programmer, or a mathematician is the feeling of triumph that comes with solving and making things in the air, from the air, by the exertion of pure imagination (and sometimes caffeine). I find it extremely fascinating that I can create a work of art which didn’t exist one morning, and it is in and of itself fulfilling.

Asides from my interest in computer science and math, I am also very much into playing Mandolin. I spend a lot of time operating it. This rewarding experience gives me peace of mind.
